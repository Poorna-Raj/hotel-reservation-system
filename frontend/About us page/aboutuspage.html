Elephant & Nature Resort

Your Gateway to Ancient Wonders and Natural Serenity

A Journey Into Tranquility

Nestled in the heart of Sri Lanka’s ancient kingdom, Elephant & Nature Resort offers travelers a one-of-a-kind escape. Surrounded by forests and wildlife, our resort is a peaceful sanctuary where history whispers through sacred trees and elephants roam free.

Discover Ancient Anuradhapura

Stay at the heart of Sri Lanka’s cultural triangle. From our resort, you can explore a wealth of iconic sites:

  • Sigiriya Rock Fortress – a breathtaking climb to a 5th-century palace atop a giant rock.
  • Sri Maha Bodhi – the oldest historically documented tree in the world, sacred to Buddhists.
  • Ruwanwelisaya Stupa – one of the tallest ancient monuments in the world.
  • Isurumuniya Temple – famous for its rock carvings and serene atmosphere.
  • Mihintale – the cradle of Buddhism in Sri Lanka with panoramic views from the summit.

These world-renowned destinations are just a short drive away. Let us help you arrange guided tours or tuk-tuk rides for an unforgettable journey through time.

Eco-Luxury Living
